Irum Shahzadi

I am Irum, a PhD student enroled on the TRANSPONDER project. My thesis aims to assess the functional diversity of pond ecosystems and how climatic and anthropogenic factors shapes the interaction among the different communities of biodiversity, including zooplankton, macroinvertebrates and macrophytes. By combining newly collected and existing data from ponds across a broad spatial scale (latitudinal and climatic gradient) in Europe, my thesis will evaluate how ponds ecosystems can be affected by environmental factors through changes in functional diversity in the pondsâ€™ communities

I will develop my thesis on the Aquatic Ecology Group at UVic, under the supervision of Dr. Maria Cuenca and Prof. Sandra Brucet. I obtain my thesis funding through the Agaur Agency from the Generalitat of Catalunya
